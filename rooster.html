Rooster

A rooster is a proud and vibrant bird with colorful plumage, typically adorned with vibrant shades of red, green, and blue. Its striking appearance is complemented by a proud stance and a regal comb atop its head. The rooster's crow is a majestic and unmistakable sound, heralding the dawn with its clear and melodious call. It resonates through the morning air, signaling the start of a new day with its powerful yet graceful presence.
